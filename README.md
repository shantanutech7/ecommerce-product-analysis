# 🧠 E-commerce Product Analysis (AIPM Case Study)

This project demonstrates product analysis and AI product thinking using data-driven insights.

## 📌 Problem Statement  
A significant number of users add products to their cart but do not complete the purchase.

## 💼 Business Impact  
High cart abandonment leads to revenue loss and poor user experience.

## 👥 Target Users  
Online shoppers who browse products and add them to cart but fail to convert.

## ⚠️ Assumptions  
- High product prices reduce conversion  
- Poor checkout experience causes drop-offs  
- Low ratings reduce trust  

## ❓ Key Questions  
- Where are users dropping off?  
- Does price affect purchase?  
- Do ratings influence buying decisions?  

## 📊 Key Metrics  
- Conversion Rate  
- Cart Abandonment Rate  
- Revenue  

## 🔍 Insights  
- ~50% users drop after adding to cart  
- Higher-priced products show lower conversion  
- Higher-rated products perform better  

## 💡 Recommendations  
- Optimize pricing (discounts, offers)  
- Promote high-rated products  
- Simplify checkout process  
- Add trust signals (reviews, badges)  

## 🚀 Conclusion  
Improving pricing strategy, trust, and checkout experience can significantly increase conversion rate and revenue.
